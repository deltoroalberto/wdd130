Nicaragua

Nicaragua is often referred to as 'the country of lakes and volcanoes' because of its many lakes, lagoons and a string of some 40 volcanoes stretching northwest to southeast along the Pacific coast. One of the Top Places To See in Nicaragua, Ometepe island is a hidden gem and a must-see on your Central American trip. Made of a perfectly-formed strata-volcano and it’s dormant twin, complete with cloudforest. It’s the highlight of many people’s time here. It’s lush, hospitable, easy to get to and has great coffee plantations.
